Here’s a summary of the key parameters of the Module **Advances in Sports Medicine and Health Promotion II** (VSM II) 
that is offered as part of the Bachelor Degree program Bachelor of Science Sportwissenschaft Performance and Health 
at Friedrich-Schiller-University Jena.


## Course Details

The course has three components: 

1. Lectures
2. Interactive Dashboards
3. Tutorials


### Lecture

- **Name:** Exercise Immunology (VSM II)
- **Starting date:** Friday, April, 10th, 2026
- **End date:** Friday, July, 10th, 2026
- **Number of sessions:** 14
- **Location:** CZS3 HS6
- **Language:** German, English

### Interactive Dashboards
- HTML/JS-based interactive dashboards are mandatory to use as learning tools

### Tutorials
- The Shiny/Python-based App PExOL Easy Plot is an essential learning tool


## Grading

Prerequisite for admission to the module examination:
- To achieve the learning objectives of this module, regular attendance at the module's seminars and exercises is required.
- Regular attendance is defined as missing no more than 15 percent of the class time in any given session.

Module examination (VSM II) in the form of three parts:
- Exercise Immunology (written exam, 30 min, pass/fail)
- Exercise Immunology (presentation/proposal, 50%)
- Applied Sports Medicine (presentation in the form of a practical teaching demonstration, 50%)
- Each module part must be passed
